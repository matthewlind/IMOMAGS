    Eliphalet Remington I would not purchase a rifle for his son Eliphalet II, so young Remington made his own barrel and took it to a local gunsmith for finishing. Soon he had the pleasure of securing the object of his desire in a complete state. The barrel was so complete a success as to extort the praise of the expert, and young Eliphalet was so encouraged that he followed up his first effort by others. Fame of the new fabrication beagan soon to fill the country side, and the young producer found the resources, and the old forge was taxed to the utmost.
